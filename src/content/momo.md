My sweet Momo,
The last few days have been really, really hard. You know, I haven't cried this much since my mother died, and I can't seem to stop crying my heart out, no matter how much I try. The silence in the house is heavy, and every corner reminds me of you.
I keep looking at your photos as I remember our last months together. Even when things were getting difficult, we found ways to share so much joy. 
You will be forever missed, Momo. I simply cannot fill the hole inside my chest that your absence has caused, and I don't think I ever truly will. A piece of my heart went with you.
I am so incredibly glad you chose us to be your family. My only hope is that we managed to make you feel completely safe, at home, and deeply loved in the short time we had together. You deserved the world, and I tried to give you all of it.
Thank you for the love you gave me. Rest in peace, my little one. ❤️
